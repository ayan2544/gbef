Limited sponsorship opportunities for GBEF events are available, with priority given to GBEF member companies. Sponsorships are customized to complement both the agenda and sponsor interests.

Most sponsorships include four components:

  • Digital and onsite visibility
  • Registration
  • Food and beverage activity
  • Direct participation (limited and as agreed between GBEF and sponsor)

For information and to discuss participation, please contact GBEF Vice President Laura Hudson at laura.hudson@gbef.tech
