Highly Interactive Executive Roundtable Agenda

  • The pre-plenary Executive Roundtable agenda opens EDGE23 and is the catalyst for substantive connections throughout the conference and beyond.
  • Three sessions of three Executive Roundtables each – 9 subjects total
  • Six participants on each Roundtable – mission and technology leaders from the United States and select US partner nations
  • 90-minutes each, with the first 15-minutes a “mini reception” allowing attendees and direct Roundtable participants to connect.
  • Public and private sector moderators invite attendee participation and input throughout the Roundtables’ duration.
  • Off-the-record, neither recorded nor broadcast, Executive Roundtables offer attendees and participants opportunities to share perspectives on various technology and mission issues candidly.

Provocative, Impactful Plenary Presentations

  • Top leaders from government and business: US Federal, American state and local, US partner nations, recognized tech innovators.
  • Current challenges and opportunities with an eye on what’s on the near-term horizon
  • Utilize state-of-the-art multimedia technologies

EDGE23 Plenary sessions are recorded in 4K and broadcast to a virtual audience that is global in its reach.

Dramatic Plenary Staging

EDGE23 Plenary sessions are on the Dolby Live Theater stage at the Park MGM Hotel, the venue for such acts as Cher, Bruno Mars, and Lady Gaga.

  • One of the world’s largest and most technologically advanced venues.
  • The LED wall is 140 feet wide and more than 50 feet tall – a side wing on each side of the stage measures half the main stage, allowing live, remote participation, and rich multimedia presentations to be included.
  • First-of-its-kind, fully integrated Dolby Atmos audio system with 402 speakers, 240 feet wide projection surface with projection mapping technology, and nine HD and 4K Projectors.

Live before the 400 in-person attendees, EDGE23 plenary sessions also are recorded and broadcast in 4K to a virtual audience that is global in its reach, and are posted on the conference and GBEF websites, and are available to speakers to repurpose.

About EDGE

The glue that binds the GBEF agenda, and distinguishes the organization from so many others, is its production of the government technology sector’s premier, most exclusive annual summit, EDGE.

With its 17-year record, EDGE enjoys recognition for its executive-level attendance, provocative content and mix of activities – all designed to enable discussions and relationships that make a difference in how technology is applied to government missions.

Limited Attendance

primarily by invitation, EDGE ensures the right mix of government and industry leaders, consistent with the conference theme, and the corresponding technology issues and missions..

Plenary Unlike Any Other

the EDGE plenary sessions are a production unlike any other conference in the sector. From the stage to the speakers to the receptions that follow, the CES Government plenary mixes elected, appointed and career government officials from around the world with the top names in technology.

Executive Roundtables

​EDGE begins with a series of Executive Roundtables. Attendees are encouraged to join in discussions on timely technologies, trends, and the missions they support.
